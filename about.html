About FormPilot

We're fixing one of banking's
most persistent frustrations

Every day, customers walk into branches with incorrectly filled forms. Every day, account officers send them home to try again. We decided that was worth fixing.

Our mission

Make every bank form submission successful, the first time

Banking in Nigeria involves a lot of paperwork. Account opening, KYC updates, mandates, indemnity forms — the list goes on. These forms were designed to be filled by hand, and the instructions are rarely clear enough for the average customer.

FormPilot exists to bridge that gap. We give account officers a simple tool to send their customers a guided, intelligent form experience — one that produces a correctly filled, ready-to-submit PDF every single time, without storing a single byte of sensitive data.

The problem

Forms are broken for everyone involved

The friction isn't laziness — it's poor design. Bank forms were built for a different era and haven't kept up with how people actually communicate and work today.

😤
Customers leave fields blank or wrong Confusing labels, tiny print, unclear instructions — errors are almost inevitable.
🔁
Multiple branch visits Officers reject forms, customers go home, come back, make new errors.
🧾
Print, scan, print again Even when forms are available digitally, the experience is still painful.
⏱️
Wasted officer time Account officers spend valuable hours correcting forms instead of serving customers.
Our approach

Guided, private, and built around real workflows

We don't replace the bank's form — we make it easier to fill correctly. The output is always the original bank PDF, placed precisely, ready for submission.

Critically, we never store your customer's data. The PDF is generated entirely in the customer's browser. Your server never sees it. Neither does ours.

And because account officers are the ones who know which forms they need most, our community lets them request and vote on what gets built next — so the form library grows around real demand.

What we stand for

Our values

🔒

Privacy by design

Customer data is never our business. We architect every feature so that sensitive information never leaves the customer's device.

🎯

Precision over polish

A beautiful interface that produces a wrong PDF is useless. We obsess over accuracy first — the form must be accepted when submitted.

🤝

Built with the community

Account officers know their work better than we do. We listen, take requests seriously, and build what's actually needed.

💡

Simplicity wins

Banking can be complicated. Our job is to absorb that complexity so neither the officer nor the customer has to feel it.

🇳🇬

Made for Nigeria

Designed around how Nigerian banking actually works — the forms, the processes, the people, the devices customers actually use.

⚡

Move fast, stay reliable

We ship new form templates quickly based on demand, without compromising the accuracy that account officers and their customers depend on.
