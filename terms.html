2. Service description

FormPilot is a software-as-a-service platform that allows account officers at Nigerian banks and financial institutions to:

  • Generate guided, step-by-step digital form links for their customers
  • Send those links via copy, WhatsApp, or email
  • Receive a notification when a customer completes a form
  • Access a library of digitised Nigerian bank forms
  • Participate in a community of banking professionals

Customers who receive form links can fill forms interactively and download a completed PDF. FormPilot does not connect to any bank's internal systems and is not affiliated with any bank.
