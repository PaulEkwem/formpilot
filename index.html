Features

Built around the real problem

✨

Guided, step-by-step experience

Customers see one question at a time. No wall of fields. Irrelevant questions are skipped automatically based on their answers.

🔒

Zero data stored

The PDF is generated entirely in the customer's browser. Nothing is saved on our servers. Full privacy by design.

📱

Works on any device

Phone, tablet, or laptop. No app download needed. Just a link.

⚡

Instant PDF output

Answers are placed precisely on the original bank form layout. No reformatting, no rejected forms.

🔔

Completion notifications

You get a ping when your customer finishes — without seeing their data.

🌍

Community-driven form library

Account officers vote on which forms to add next. New banks and form types are added regularly based on real demand.
