# GTBank Referee Form — Layout Reference

Source: Screenshot provided by Paul Ekwem (GTBank official referee letter)

## Header
- Top-left: "To: / The Manager, / Guaranty Trust Bank Ltd"
- Top-right: GTCO orange logo box + "Guaranty Trust Bank Ltd / RC 152521"
- Salutation: "Dear Sir,"

## Fields (in order)

1. **Name of Company** — blank line
2. **"I/We would wish to confirm that the know the above-named Company and its Directors for"** — blank line (how long they've known them)
3. **"I/We would like to comment about their suitability for maintaining a current account with yourselves as follows:"** — 3–4 blank lines (comment area)
4. **"I/We maintain a current account with:"**
   - Name of Bank: ____________
   - Address: ____________
5. **My/Our Account No. is:** — 10 boxes (account number grid)
6. **And my/our phone No. (s) is/are:** — blank line
7. **"Yours faithfully,"**
8. **Signature line** — blank + label "Signature"
9. **Date** — Day / Month / Year boxes
10. **Name** — blank line
11. **Address** — blank line
12. **Name of Director known to the referee** — blank line

## Please Note (printed footer):
1. Referees must be a current account holder either in GTCO or any other bank. Referees from other banks must be corporate account holders only.
2. Referee's account must not be less than six months old.
3. Salary account holder(s) are not suitable referees.
